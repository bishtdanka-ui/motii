What We Do

Three ways we get you moving.

01 / NEW INSTALLATION

Ground-up elevator installation

Shaft assessment, machine selection, and full installation for new residential or commercial buildings — coordinated with your architect and site team.

02 / CUSTOM FITTING

Cab, door & panel fit-out

Interior fitting for cabs, doors, and control panels — matched to your building's finish, load requirements, and accessibility needs.

03 / RETROFIT & MODERNIZATION

Upgrade an existing lift

Replace ageing mechanicals or controls in an existing shaft without a full rebuild — minimising downtime for the building.
